About Ashley

Cake Coven Services was built for real homes, real lives, and the people who need an extra set of hands without judgment.

Hi, I'm Ashley.

Cake Coven Services exists because people deserve support that is practical, respectful, and kind. Whether your home needs a deep clean, a room needs organizing, or the holidays need decorating before you run out of time, I want the process to feel easier from the moment you ask.

My goal is not to judge your space. My goal is to notice the details, create a plan, and help you walk back into a home that feels lighter.

We believe every detail matters so you can breathe easier.
